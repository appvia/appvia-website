Appvia Hub

What is the Appvia Hub?

Appvia Hub is our core product, a complete solution for building and managing your apps independently from each other – however many you plan to run at the same time.

It’s a self-service developer platform that promotes a modern Agile approach, using cloud computing and containers to decouple your apps from the environment in which they run, to create highly portable, easily scalable software.

The result? Appvia Hub takes the load of infrastructure management off your developers’ shoulders. It also gives them all the resources they need to build and deploy software more efficiently. So all your efforts can go into adding value for your customers.

Create a powerful platform for your app

  • Ideal for initial setup or migration
  • Cloud agnostic – manage your apps across multiple clouds and integrations
  • Includes Docker registries (Quay or ECR)
  • Creates Kubernetes clusters using those offered by cloud services

Future-proof your apps

  • Our platform creates app resilience and flexibility
  • Standardised infrastructure that’s portable and easy to scale

Stay in control

  • Have a central view on your activities within a single ‘space’
  • Monitoring dashboards report on performance
  • SSO authentication integration
  • Manage robot tokens for continuous integration (CI) pipelines

Give your developers everything they need

  • Git repositories with a full suite of tools
  • Talks to GitHub and SaaS products

Best practices built in for your security

  • Rigorous performance testing through all standard testing cycles
  • Best practices enforced by overrides where necessary
  • Helps identify vulnerabilities across your Kubernetes clusters
  • Enforces security through the lifecycle of your app

Support to suit you

  • 24/7 service by phone and live chat
  • Choice of email support response times, from 24 hours to 1 hour
  • You manage the status and priority of support tickets
  • We can provide an on-site engineer and training
