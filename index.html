Services

Appvia provides application hosting consultancy services.

We help you succeed by understanding your business requirements first. At Appvia, solutions come after requirements. We use the practise of Software Reliability Engineering.

Cloud

We are experts in Amazon Web Services, and have run projects using Google Cloud Platform. We also have capability with Openstack.

We are cloud native and aim to provide the best hosting options for your organisation.

Kubernetes

We have years of experience running multi tenant Kubernetes clusters in production.

We are active contributors to the kops project which enables us to effectivly manage Kubernetes.

Security

We understand security best practises and latest patterns for proactive security messures at every stage of your application lifecycle.

We ensure that trust exists in all the complex software layers that underpin any large suite of software services.

Open Source

We choose open source first. We provide scalable engineering expertise by contributing and using open source software. We are also realistic and pragmatic and have close links and experience with comercial software providers for when they provide the best fit your business requirements.

Consulting

Although our primary services are around engineering, we understand that for us to succeed we need to fully understand our clients requirements first.

Rest assured, we will not lead you into a technical cul-de-sac, if you succeed, we succeed.

Training
