Services

Appvia provides technical transformation services to help deliver innovative and simple solutions to your business.

We help you succeed by taking the time to truly understand your business requirements, to make sure the solutions that follow are flexible, simple and effective. We use the practice of Site Reliability Engineering, in conjunction with DevOps and Agile methodologies.

We engineer self-service solutions, reducing the dependency on DevOps resources to projects. The rate of delivery is accelerated, creating a better relationship between the application developers and the operational needs of the service.

Cloud

We are experts in Amazon Web Services, and have run projects using Google Cloud Platform. We also have capability with Openstack.

We are cloud native and aim to provide the best hosting options for your organisation.

Kubernetes

We have years of experience running multi tenant Kubernetes clusters in production.

We are active contributors to the kops project which enables us to effectively manage Kubernetes.

Security

We understand security best practices, and the latest patterns for proactive security measures at every stage of your application lifecycle.

We ensure that trust exists in all the complex software layers that underpin any large suite of software services.

Open Source

We provide scalable engineering expertise by selecting, creating and contributing to open source software solutions. We are also realistic, pragmatic and have close links and experience with commercial software providers for when they provide the best fit for your business requirements.

Consulting

Although our primary services are around engineering, we understand that for us to succeed, we first need to fully understand our clients requirements.

Rest assured, we will not lead you into a technical cul-de-sac, if you succeed, we succeed.

Support and Training

We will support what we deliver when services enter a production lifecycle, to offer the assurance your services need. We will also consult and mentor your application teams and ensure that all application hosting services are fully understood.
